Milestone 8 (OpenLayers Mapping)

Q1:

What area do you want to depict in your map? Why?

A:

Santa Barbara, California.

Q2:

What is the center point (latitude and longitude) of your area of interest?

A:

34° 25' 33"N 119° 42' 51"W. (34.425833, -119.714167)

Q3:

What base map(s) did you select for use in your map? Why?

A:

OpenStreetMap (OSM) and Bing. I chose these services because I wanted to experiment with services other than Google's.

Q4:

What is the scale of your map (local, regional, continental, global)? How will this translate into your selection of an appropriate default zoom level for your map?

A:

Local scale. I felt that "zoom level" 13 was appropriate for this scale because it focuses mostly on Santa Barbara rather than the surrounding areas.

Q5:

Embed the OpenLayers Map in your writeup that is based upon your responses to questions 1-4 above.

A:


Santa Barbara, CA
