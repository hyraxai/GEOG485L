Milestone 3 (Web-based Mapping Clients: Google Maps API - Part I)

Q1:

What area do you want to depict in your map? Why?

A:

I want to log all of the WiFi nodes that I encounter in a one (1) week time span. This process is known as "wardriving". Using a free and open source (FOSS) application on my Android based smartphone, I am able to log every network that I encounter, complete with GPS coordinates of where I first pick up the broadcast of a given signal.

I think that all of the data collected using this process would create an interesting map.

Q2:

What is the center point (latitude and longitude) of your area of interest?

A:

Coordinates: 35° 06' 20.04"N 106° 37' 45.41"W.

Q3:

What style of map (roads, satellite, hybrid, terrain) is appropriate for your map? Why?

A:

I believe that a road map would be best, as this activity is performed primarily while driving or walking.

Q4:

What is the scale of your map (local, regional, continental, global)? How will this translate into your selection of an appropriate default zoom level for your map?

A:

Local scale. I would like the initial zoom level to have all of the data points visible.

Embed a Google Map in your writeup that is based upon your responses to questions 1-4 above.

A:
